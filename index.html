Who is Ken ?

  • Ken is a doll created by the Mattel company in 1961.
  • He serves as the boyfriend of Barbie, the famous fashion doll.
  • Over the years, he has experienced a lot of stylistic adaptations, showcasing changing fashion trends.
  • Ken dolls come with lots of different outfits, showing off different jobs and styles.
